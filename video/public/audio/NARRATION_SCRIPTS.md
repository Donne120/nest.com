# Nest Tutorial Narration Scripts

Each script maps to one MP3 file saved in this folder.
Timing guide: speak at a natural pace — roughly 130 words per minute.
Each video is ~26–30 seconds. Leave a brief 0.5s pause between steps.

---

## T01_Welcome.mp3
**"Welcome to Nest"** · ~28 seconds

> Welcome to Nest — your organisation's learning platform.
>
> After logging in, you'll land on your Dashboard. This shows your enrolled courses, recent activity, and progress at a glance.
>
> To see all available courses, click "My Courses" in the sidebar. The platform highlights how far along you are in each one.
>
> Click any course to open it and see the full list of videos — with your progress shown for each one. Let's get started.

---

## T02_OrgSetup.mp3
**"Setting Up Your Organisation"** · ~28 seconds

> Let's set up your Nest organisation.
>
> From the sidebar, click "Settings" to open your workspace configuration. Here you can update your organisation's name, website, and industry.
>
> Head to the Branding section to upload your logo. This appears on all learner-facing pages, giving your workspace a professional feel.
>
> When you're done, click "Save Changes". Your settings go live immediately across your entire workspace.

---

## T03_ManageTeam.mp3
**"Managing Your Team"** · ~30 seconds

> Let's look at how to manage your team.
>
> Click "Team" in the sidebar to see every member of your organisation — their role, email, and whether they're active or pending.
>
> To bring someone new in, click "Invite Member", enter their email, choose their role — Learner or Educator — and send the invitation. They'll get a link to join straight away.
>
> You can also click on any member to open their profile and update their role between Learner, Educator, or Owner at any time.

---

## T04_CreateCourse.mp3
**"Creating Courses & Videos"** · ~30 seconds

> Here's how to create a course and add your first video.
>
> Go to Courses and click "New Course". Give it a clear title and a short description — this is what your learners see when they browse.
>
> Inside the course, click "Add Video" and drag your MP4 file into the upload area. Nest processes it automatically in the background.
>
> Once uploaded, add a title and description for the video so learners know what to expect before they press play.

---

## T05_Transcription.mp3
**"Transcription & AI Q&A"** · ~28 seconds

> Nest automatically transcribes every video you upload.
>
> Open any video and click the "Transcript" tab to read the full, time-stamped transcript. You can download it as a text file too.
>
> Switch to the "Q&A" tab to use the AI assistant. Type any question about the video — the AI reads the transcript and gives you a precise, referenced answer. It even tells you exactly where in the video the answer comes from.

---

## T06_Quizzes.mp3
**"Quizzes & Assessments"** · ~28 seconds

> Quizzes help you check that learners actually understood the content.
>
> As an educator, open any video and go to the "Quiz" tab. Click "Add Question", write your question, enter the answer options, and mark the correct one.
>
> Learners see the quiz after watching the video. Questions appear one at a time, they pick an answer, and get immediate feedback — along with an explanation of why it's correct.

---

## T07_Assignments.mp3
**"Assignments & Tasks"** · ~32 seconds

> Assignments let you set real work for your learners to complete.
>
> Go to Assignments and click "New Assignment". Add a title, instructions, a due date, and link it to a course.
>
> Learners open the assignment, read your instructions, type their response or upload a file, and click Submit. It's that simple.
>
> As an educator, you'll see all submissions listed. Click any one to read it, leave written feedback, and either approve it or request a revision.

---

## T08_Progress.mp3
**"Progress Tracking"** · ~26 seconds

> Nest keeps detailed progress records for every learner.
>
> Head to the Progress page to see your overall completion rate, videos watched, and time spent — broken down by course.
>
> As an educator, go to any learner's profile under Team to see their individual progress across every course — including the last time they were active.

---

## T09_Meetings.mp3
**"1-on-1 Meetings"** · ~28 seconds

> Nest has built-in 1-on-1 meetings so learners and educators can connect directly.
>
> Click "Schedule Meeting", pick a date on the calendar, choose an available time slot, and confirm. The other person receives a calendar invite automatically.
>
> When it's time, click "Join" next to the meeting. Nest opens a video call right in your browser — no extra app needed.

---

## T10_Analytics.mp3
**"Analytics & Reporting"** · ~28 seconds

> The Analytics page gives you a complete picture of how your organisation is learning.
>
> You'll see total video views, active learners, average completion rates, and quizzes taken — all in one place. You can export everything as a CSV.
>
> Click on any course to drill deeper. You'll see which videos are most watched, average watch time, and where learners are dropping off — so you know what to improve.

---

## T11_Certificates.mp3
**"Certificates"** · ~26 seconds

> Nest automatically issues certificates when learners complete a course.
>
> Once you've watched all the videos and passed any quizzes, your certificate appears in the Certificates section. Click to view it — it shows your name, the course, and the completion date.
>
> Download it as a PDF to save it, or use the Share Link to add it directly to your LinkedIn profile.

---

## T12_Payments.mp3
**"Payments & Billing"** · ~30 seconds

> Let's walk through payments and billing.
>
> Go to Billing to choose a plan — Starter for up to ten learners, Professional for up to fifty, or Enterprise for unlimited. Click "Choose Plan" on the one that fits your organisation.
>
> After making a bank transfer, come back and upload your payment receipt. Your admin reviews it and activates your plan — usually within minutes.
>
> Under the History tab you can view all past payments and download invoices for your records.

---

## Recording Tips

- Speak clearly at a relaxed, natural pace
- Record in a quiet room with minimal echo
- Export as MP3, 128kbps or higher, stereo or mono
- Name files exactly as shown (e.g. `T01_Welcome.mp3`) and place them in this folder
- The video will fade the audio in/out automatically — no need to add fades yourself
